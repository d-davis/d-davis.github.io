Code and Datasets

Scientific progress requires transparency. Listed below are links to data repositories where code and other necessary data can be found to replicate the analyses discussed in my publications. The data are ordered by region and in reverse chronological order

Africa

2025

Data and R-Code for Davis et al. in review, Scientific Reports, available here.

2022

Data and R-Code for Davis et al. 2023, Antiquity, available here.

2021

Javascript and R-Code for Davis & Douglass 2021, Frontiers in Ecology and Evolution, available here.

2020

R-code and datasets* for Davis et al. 2020, Geosciences: https://www.mdpi.com/2076-3263/10/8/287/s1 R-code and datasets for Davis et al. 2020, Journal of Archaeological Science available here.

North America

2021

Trained Mask R-CNN model and R-code for Davis et al. 2021, Journal of Archaeological Science, available here

2020

R-code and datasets* for Davis et al. 2020, Advances in Archaeological Practice, available here.

2019

Datasets* for Davis et al. 2019, Journal of Archaeological Science Reports, available here. Datasets* for Davis et al. 2019, Southeastern Archaeology, available here.

Europe

2021

Trained RetinaNet model for Davis & Lundin 2021, Remote Sensing, available here.

*Note that due to legal restrictions and/or ethical concerns, archaeological site locations are not included in this publically available data. All other necessary information to replicate analyses are provided.

Oceania

2024

Rapa Nui (Easter Island): Datasets for Davis et al. 2024, Science Advances, available at Dryad.

2022

Samoa: Supporting Data for Quintus et al. 2023, Archaeological Prospection, available at Zenodo.
